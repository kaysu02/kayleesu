Kendra Scott Design Project

Kendra Scott is a women-led jewelry company that values family, fashion, and philanthropy.

Roles: Product Designer & Researcher

Challenge

Customization in the jewelry industry allows for the development of a personal experience with one's jewelry. Design a jewelry piece which is marketable towards the Kendra Scott consumer while considering the theme of personalization to encompass both the jewelry and the purchasing experience.

Consumer Research

We created a survey to obtain general demographic information and customer preference data, and conduced a focus group with 5 participants to understand how to bring in new customers to Kendra Scott through creating designs that bring value to the participants.

  • Varying age groups
  • Average technical skills
  • Primary shoppers
  • Mix of in-store and digital customers

Survey Questions Focus Group Insights

Key Findings

  • Jewelry serves as an identity piece
  • People want the human interaction when customizing
  • The experiential process is important and valuable
  • Purchases influenced by trends on social media and on the street

    Competitive Analysis

    We chose to compare Kendra Scott Jewelry to 3 other brands: Pandora, James Avery, and Mejuri as they are popular options among this customer group.

    Final Sketches and Line Architecture

    Our designs are mainly customizable through the Kendra Scott colorbar which allows for customers to choose their own stone colors when the jewelry is created. This aligns with our consumer reseearch as participants were most interested in color customization. Our participants also favored lower prices (under $70) with a gold finish, which requires gold plating material. Some pieces are in gold vermeil for engraving purposes as an extra step in customization.

    3D Prototypes

    We prototyped our designs in Fusion360 and 3D printed them to test the scaling of our products along with their "look and feel" as 3D models.

    Social Media & Product Launch

    Chosen Piece

    After conversing with the Kendra Scott designers, they chose our asymmetrical drop earring which was revised into the Senna design due to feasibility concerns for the original design. The remodeled earring now encompasses the iconic "Danielle" shape which better aligns with Kendra Scott's image.

    Customer Experience

    We connected standard aura colors to Kendra Scott stones that are available at the color bar so that customers can easily connect with a certain stone and customize their jewelry with it

    The highlight for the Senna earring launch party would be Aura Photography where your aura is captured using electical sensors and translated into color. Kendra Scott consumers will be able to take home both their new jewelry piece and a matching photograph with their aura and stone color.

    The custom aura quiz is available for those who cannot attend the event in-person and for future use as customers can continue to build their realtionship with jewelry through their aura

    The Snapchat filter is available for those unable to attend in-person and can be used to further boost social media recognition

    Closing

    Our Group's design was chosen by Kendra Scott executives to launch in stores!
